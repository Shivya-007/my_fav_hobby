How to Get Started

  1. Warm Up

    Start by gently warming up your vocal cords. Humming, lip trills, and sirens are great exercises.

  2. Breath Control

    Learn diaphragmatic breathing. Proper breath support is essential for singing.

  3. Pitch Practice

    Use a piano or a pitch app to practice hitting different notes accurately.

  4. Vocal Exercises

    Explore scales, arpeggios, and vocal exercises to strengthen your voice.

  5. Song Selection

    Choose songs that match your vocal range. Start with simpler tunes.

  6. Record Yourself

    Record your singing to track progress and identify areas for improvement.
